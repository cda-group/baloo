Welcome to Baloo's documentation!
=================================

Baloo implements the `bare necessities <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=08NlhjpVFsU>`_
of `Pandas <https://pandas.pydata.org/>`_ with the *lazy* evaluating
and optimizing `Weld <https://github.com/weld-project/weld>`_ framework.
